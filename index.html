About Me

Welcome! I am Santosh Kumar Gupta, a dedicated researcher in Educational Data Mining. I hold degrees of M.Sc. and M.Ed. in Science and Education. My passion lies in exploring the vast potential of data mining techniques to enhance educational outcomes and contribute to the field of educational research.

Research

My research focuses on the application of data mining techniques to uncover patterns and insights in educational data. By analyzing large datasets, I aim to improve teaching methods, enhance student learning experiences, and support data-driven decision-making in education.
